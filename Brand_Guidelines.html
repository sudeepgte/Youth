Brand Guidelines

Design System & Identity for the Youth (Zentrix) Project

1. Core Theme & Concept

The visual identity revolves around a "Cool Blue / Ice Blue / Cyan Glow" aesthetic. It embraces modern glassmorphism (frosted glass effects), floating elements, and vibrant glowing accents against deep, high-contrast backgrounds to establish a premium and dynamic feel.

3. Typography

The primary font family is Outfit, chosen for its clean, geometric, and modern feel.

Outfit Bold (800)

Outfit Regular (400) - Used for body text and paragraphs. It provides excellent readability while maintaining the modern aesthetic of the brand.

Glowing Text Effect

Used for emphasis on dark backgrounds

4. Imagery & Backgrounds

Backgrounds utilize deep blues and fluid wave structures to add depth and motion to the interface without distracting from the core content.
