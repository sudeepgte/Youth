Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Zentrix platform, your account, and our features.

What is Zentrix?

Zentrix is a comprehensive community platform for students and creators. It offers real-time chat, event management, social features like reels and posts, and a variety of online games to keep the community engaged.

How do I earn Zentrix Coins?

You can earn Zentrix Coins by daily login, participating in events, voting on polls, and engaging with the community. These coins can be spent in the Reward Shop to unlock exclusive features and badges.

How can I register for an event?

Navigate to the Events page, find an event you're interested in, and click "Book Now". Follow the registration steps, and you'll receive a digital ticket once confirmed.

What are the different post types?

Zentrix supports standard Posts (text/image), Reels (short videos), and Thoughts (testimonials). You can share these from your dashboard or profile to interact with your followers.

Is Zentrix free to use?

Yes, Zentrix is free to use for basic communication and community participation. Some premium events or exclusive features may require Zentrix Coins or a small registration fee.
